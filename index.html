Stuck enroute?

We at RoadX bridge the gap between citizens who are in need of a mechanic, and local garages.

Our customers can be anyone, from a person stuck in the middle of nowhere to a salary earning individual who needs to get their vechile serviced.

How We Work?

REQUEST

The user generates the request for service or repair of the vehicle.

ACCEPT

Once the request is received by the garage, the request gets accepted by one of them.

DESPATCH & DELIVERY

The vechile is picked up from the location and is delivered back to the customer after the request is completed.

Why Choose Us?

Affordable

We connect the users to the nearest certified mechanics which offer services at a cheaper rate when compared to the OEM Service Stations.

Pick and Drop Service

The garages we have partenered with offer door step delivery. Also, the service also extends to roadside assistance for the times when you are stuck in the middle of road.
